## Design Context

### Users
Spanish-speaking professionals and enthusiasts interested in AI, automation, and productivity. They discover Alquimia through word-of-mouth or social media, land on the page, and decide whether to join the free Discord community. Context: browsing on desktop or phone, likely evening/night (dark theme is native to the use case).

### Brand Personality
**Mystical, scholarly, artisanal** — the interface evokes an alchemist's workshop: careful craft, arcane knowledge made accessible, the transformation of raw material into gold. Not exclusive or gatekeepy — the community is open and free.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Dark, warm, gold/sepia palette. Old-world scholarly with alchemical symbolism. Noise texture, vignette, decorative corner frames.
- **Typography**: Cormorant Garamond (display), EB Garamond (body), IM Fell English SC (ornamental labels), JetBrains Mono (mono). User confirmed these are intentional choices despite appearing on reflex lists.
- **Theme**: Dark — derived from the alchemical concept and evening browsing context.
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS landing pages, AI slop aesthetics (cyan-on-dark, purple gradients, glassmorphism).

### Design Principles
1. **Craft over template** — every element should feel hand-placed, not generated from a grid system
2. **Restraint creates power** — gold accents work because they're rare; whitespace speaks
3. **Motion with purpose** — animate to reveal, guide, and delight, never to decorate
4. **Warmth through darkness** — the dark palette should feel inviting, like candlelight, not cold
5. **Respect the reader** — accessible contrast, generous touch targets, reduced-motion support
